About Me

Hi, I'm Muhammad Wasim, a passionate and detail-oriented Full-Stack Developer with expertise in building scalable and efficient web applications. I specialize in modern web technologies like Laravel, React, Node.js, and MongoDB, and I love turning complex problems into simple, elegant solutions.

I hold a Bachelor's degree in Software Engineering from City University of Science & IT, Peshawar. Over the years, I've worked on a variety of projects, from small personal tools to large-scale platforms like LEGSER (a legal services platform) and a Student Attendance Management System.

My journey in software development began in 2021 when I started learning the basics of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. Since then, I've expanded my skill set to include backend development with Laravel and Node.js, as well as frontend development with React and Tailwind CSS.

I'm passionate about creating user-friendly, responsive, and high-performance applications. Whether it's building a RESTful API, designing a sleek UI, or optimizing database queries, I enjoy every aspect of the development process. My goal is to continuously learn and grow as a developer while contributing to meaningful projects that make a difference.

When I'm not coding, you can find me exploring new technologies, contributing to open-source projects, or sharing my knowledge through blogs and tutorials. I believe in the power of collaboration and am always open to new opportunities and challenges.
